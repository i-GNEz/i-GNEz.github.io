Plot The Plot

An event about designing a basic 2d plan of a building floor and its elements, thus, testing your skills in designing.

7 May , 2021

2:30 PM

Rules and Regulations

1. You have to share your screen at the time of making floor plan.
2. Participants have to upload their PDF and SOURCE file.
3. Design must contain basic building floor plan and its elements.
4. Participants can decide the number of doors, windows and other building characteristics in the design that don’t violates our parameters.
5. Participants can use Any Software for designing the floor plan, from AutoCAD, FreeCAD, Blender, 3D Max to Paint.
6. Design will be judged on the basis of Utility, Cleanliness, Measurements, etc.
Note: - Parameters will be disclosed though What’s app Group/Emails.
